Masters of Orion IV: Decision Optimization

Factors that Play into Decision Making

Masters of Orion is a comprehensive strategy game involving many variables which impact the many paths to victory. While some paths to victory may seem subjective and hard to measure, all aspects of the game involve some mathematics. Some mathematics are much easier and important to discern than others. In the case of Masters of Orion IV diplomacy and espionage are shrouded in unknown equations and probabilities whereas production, population, finances, and science are much more observable, controllable, and in many respects more important. That isn't to say that ignoring espionage and diplomacy goes without consequence, but it is the economy of your empire that dirves it's success. Because of this my website will focus on the mathematical and relational aspects of these elements where they originate, colony management. Once the corresponding mathematical relationships are fully examined a marginal benefit vs marginal cost framework can be established to help determine which actions arrive at better outcomes. Once that is established then a framework that allows for patronization of certain factors (industry, population growth, science, ships, etc.) can be more fully extrapolated. The outcomes expressed here should be taken as advice and measures for decision making rather than a substitute for it as there are various factors colony management that drive an empire forward.

Colonies

Colonies are the centerpiece to Masters of Orion. They drive the economical, industrial and scientific capabilities of every empire. Indeed no player can win the game without having effective colonies to finance, build and progress their empire. Colonies have various aspects to them which interrelate in various ways. A break down into each of these aspects and how they relate to one another shown in the lists below.
