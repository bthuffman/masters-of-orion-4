Masters of Orion IV: Decision Optimization

Factors that Play into Decision Making

Masters of Orion is a comprehensive strategy game involving many variables which impact the many paths to victory. While some paths to victory may seem subjective and hard to measure, all aspects of the game involve some mathematics. Some mathematics are much easier and important to discern than others. In the case of Masters of Orion IV diplomacy and espionage are shrouded in unknown equations and probabilities whereas colony management, finances, and science are much more observable and controllable. Because of this my website will focus on the mathematical aspects that affect colonies, finance and science.

Colonies

Colonies are the centerpiece to Masters of Orion. They drive the economical, industrial and scientific capabilities of every empire. Indeed no player can win the game without having effective colonies to finance, build and progress their empire. Colonies have various aspects to them which interrelate in various ways. A break down into each of these aspects and how they relate to one another shown in the lists below.

Colony Foundations

These describe the attributes that come with each colony.

  • Environmental Class: adjustable with runaway climage change or terraforming.
  • Mineral Class: adjusted occasionally by Galactic New Network Random Events
  • Size Class
  • Population: adjustable with bombardment and population growth
